MT Marathon in the Americas 2024 Venue


This year MT Marathon in the Americas will take place in Baltimore, MD at Johns Hopkins University (3400 N. Charles Street). Most rooms will be in Hackerman Hall.

Transportation

Baltimore is easily accessible from BWI via the MARC train. Tickets can be purchased at the train station or on the CharmPass App. This app will also provide tickets to local busses.

Hopkins provides shuttles from the train station and Mt. Vernon to Homewood Campus.
