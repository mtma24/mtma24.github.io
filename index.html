MT Marathon


The Machine Translation Marathon in the Americas 2022 will take place from July 18th through July 22nd in Redmond, WA, and is organised by Microsoft Translator.

We invite researchers, developers, students and users of machine translation to participate in the MT Marathon in the Americas 2022, which will feature:

  • MT Lectures covering the basics and tutorials.
  • Keynote Talks from experienced researchers and practitioners.
  • Hacking Projects to advance tools or research in one week or start new collaborations.
